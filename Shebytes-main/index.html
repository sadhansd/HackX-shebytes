Rights

Gender identity

Transgender people have a constitutional right to change their legal gender and a third gender is recognised

Recognition of relationships

Legal status of same-sex unions. Minimal recognition in India. (same-sex marriage under consideration)

Adoption

Adoption by single LGBT people recognized. adoption by one partner of a same-sex couple of the other's biological child (step-child adoption), or adoption by a single LGBT+ person.

Discrimination protections

Sexual orientation and gender identity protections nationwide. It is designed to prevent discrimination in employment, housing, education, and other areas of social life.
